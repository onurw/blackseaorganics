Our Story

BlackSea Organics is a micro-producer from Turkey’s Black Sea mountains. We work with foragers and smallholder families to craft limited releases: Lazberry (Karayemiş), wild bilberries and a ritual white tea.

Every jar or pouch is touched by real hands — handpicked, gently dried, beautifully packed. That’s why we produce less, but better.

Spring

White tea buds are handpicked at sunrise. Slow drying preserves delicate notes.

Summer

Wild bilberries and Lazberry are foraged on cool mornings, then sun-dried in small trays.

Autumn

Jam batches are prepared in glass, with minimal sugar and no artificial pectin.

All Year

We pack in glass first, ship worldwide and keep batches traceable.
